Welcome to Christ for the Deliverance Empowerment Initiative

Christ for the Deliverance Empowerment Initiative (CDEI),

we are called to heal, uplift, and empower. We are a faith-based, humanitarian nonprofit organization committed to transforming lives through a blend of spiritual nurture, education, social welfare, and economic empowerment.

We exist to ensure that every woman, child, and vulnerable person has access to dignity, opportunity, and a future filled with hope. Whether through teaching, training, feeding, or overseas study support—we’re devoted to raising godly, impactful individuals.

What We Do

✝️ Our Mission

To transform lives by delivering hope, teaching godly values, and empowering the vulnerable through spiritual, educational, and humanitarian programs.

🌍 Our Vision

A world where every woman, child, and community can rise above hardship, walk in faith, and live empowered by God’s love.

💡 What We Do

our work is rooted in compassion, faith, and a deep commitment to restoring dignity to the lives of the underserved. Through our diverse programs, we seek to not only meet immediate needs but also build long-term transformation by equipping people with the tools, knowledge, and faith to rise into their God-given purpose.
👩‍👧

Women & Children Empowerment

We provide training, micro-grants, mentorship, and resources to empower women and children toward self-sufficiency and dignity.
